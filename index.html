Circulatory System • April 2024

Circulatory System

The Circulatory System transports blood from the heart to the lungs for oxygenation, then distributes oxygen-rich blood throughout the body via arteries. Veins return oxygen-depleted blood to the heart, maintaining the circulation cycle essential for the health of organs, muscles, and tissues.

The circulatory system comprises your heart and blood vessels. Its primary role is to deliver oxygen, nutrients, and hormones to muscles, tissues, and organs across your body. Additionally, it aids in eliminating waste from cells and organs, facilitating their disposal by the body.

The circulatory system can also be referred to as your cardiovascular system. Cardio means heart, and vascular refers to blood vessels.

Respiratory System • April 2024

Respiratory System

The respiratory system is the biological system in organisms that enables breathing and exchange of gases between the body and the environment. It includes several organs and structures involved in the process of respiration, which is essential for supplying oxygen to the body's cells and removing carbon dioxide, a waste product of cellular metabolism.

The different organs of the respiratory system such as the nose, larynx, trachea, bronchi, etc. carry out the process of respiration/breathing. All these organs play an important role in supplying oxygen to the body and allowing its smooth functioning.

Respiratory and Circulatory System

The cardiovascular system, or circulatory system, circulates blood throughout your body, collaborating with your respiratory system to deliver oxygen-rich blood to your cells. Breathing commences as you inhale air through your nose or mouth, which then travels down your throat and enters the windpipe, known as the trachea, which branches into airways called bronchial tubes.

Topic • April 2024

Respiratory and Circulatory System Diseases

Respiratory diseases hinder breathing, often from infections or environmental factors, while circulatory issues, like blockages, cause symptoms such as pain and breathlessness, necessitating early intervention for both.
